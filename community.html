About

The HD Community consists of over 350,000 members from many different countries. Members come together to discuss videos and games and engage in projects through their ideas and creations. Our committed staff team of over 15 people ensure the community maintains a friendly and positive outlook.

HD Dev is one of two HD Community groups; it is home to our skilled selection of developers, and provides a platform for our 50,000+ members to discuss topics on game development and suggest ideas for upcoming projects.

Discord is a free voice and text chat app that enables easy communication between users. The HD Community Discord Server was established in 2017 and has grown to a community of over 4000 members.
