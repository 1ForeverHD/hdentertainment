HD Admin















HD Admin is a game extension developed by Ben in 2017. It enables developers to activate a wide range of commands that improves their ability to test games and enhances the experience of players. Originally HD Admin was designed to only be used for HD games but over the years has grown to over 12,000,000 sales, becoming the platform's #1 model.

Background

Our games are primarily developed in Roblox Studio, but also incorporate software such as Photoshop and Blender. A game can take from a month to seven months to initially develop, with additional updates after release.
